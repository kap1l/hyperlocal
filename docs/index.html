Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 25, 2026

App Name: MicroWeather ("the App")

1. Overview

MicroWeather is designed with a Local-First philosophy. We do not operate a backend server, we do not have a user database, and we do not collect your personal information.

2. Data Collection & Usage

Location Data

The App requests access to your location (Foreground and Background) solely to:

  • Fetch local weather forecasts.
  • Provide "Rain Alerts" and "Morning Briefings" based on your current surroundings.

Privacy Promise: Your precise coordinates are sent directly to our weather providers (PirateWeather, Open-Meteo) anonymously. They are never transmitted to MicroWeather developers or stored on any external server owned by us.

Activity Data

Your activity preferences (e.g., "Running", "Photography") and history are stored strictly on your device using secure local storage.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services to provide weather data:

  • PirateWeather API: For weather forecasts.
  • Open-Meteo API: For air quality data.

These services receive your latitude/longitude to generate the forecast. We do not share your device ID or advertising ID with them.

4. Your Rights

Since we do not store your data, there is nothing for us to delete from our servers. You can wipe all local data from your device at any time using the "Delete All App Data" button in the App Settings.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us via our GitHub repository issues page.
