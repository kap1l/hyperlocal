## Full Description
Stop guessing if the weather is good enough for your run. **OutWeather** gives you a precise "Activity Score" based on temperature, wind, rain, and humidity, so you know exactly when to head out.

**Key Features:**
🏃 **Activity Scores:** Instantly see if it's a 10/10 day for running, cycling, or hiking.
☀️ **Hyperlocal Accuracy:** Powered by PirateWeather for minute-by-minute precision.
📍 **Clean & Simple:** No clutter, just the weather data you need for your workout.
🔔 **Daily Notifications:** Get a morning report on the best time to exercise today.
🚫 **No Ads (Pro):** Enjoy a seamless experience focused purely on your outdoor lifestyle.

Whether you're training for a marathon or just want a pleasant evening jog, OutWeather helps you find the perfect window.
