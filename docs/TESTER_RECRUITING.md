# Beta Tester Recruitment Strategy & Templates

Since you need 15 more testers to hit the 20-tester minimum, the fastest and most reliable way is "Test 4 Test" on Reddit. Developers test your app, and you test theirs.

## 1. Where to post
*   **[r/AndroidClosedTesting](https://www.reddit.com/r/AndroidClosedTesting/)** - Highly active, strictly for this purpose.
*   **[r/GooglePlayDeveloper](https://www.reddit.com/r/GooglePlayDeveloper/)** - Also has tester exchange threads.

## 2. What you need before posting
Make sure you have a Google Group created and added to your Play Console. It is much easier for testers to join a group than for you to manually add 15 email addresses.
1. Create a group: `groups.google.com` (e.g., `outweather-testers`)
2. In Play Console -> Closed Testing -> Testers: Add the Google Group email.
3. Grab the **Google Group Link**, **Android Link**, and **Web Link**.

## 3. Reddit Post Template

**Title:** `[Need 15 Testers] OutWeather - A Hyperlocal Weather & Activity App. Will test yours back!`

**Body:**

```text
Hi everyone! I just launched the closed beta for my new weather app, OutWeather, and I need 15 more testers to meet the Google Play requirements. I will absolutely test your app in return! Just leave your links in the comments.

**About the App:** 
OutWeather provides hyperlocal weather forecasts and specific "Activity Scores" (Running, Cycling, Hiking) so you know exactly when it's safe and comfortable to head outside. 

**Links to Join:**
1. Join the Google Group first: **[Click to Join Group](https://groups.google.com/g/outweather-beta-tester)**
*(Your email: `outweather-beta-tester@googlegroups.com`)*
2. Join on Web: [Insert Web Join Link from Play Console]
3. Download on Android: [Insert Android Join Link from Play Console]

Please keep the app installed for the full 14 days and open it at least once. If you test mine, leave a comment with your app's links and I will install it right away and keep it for 14 days.

Thank you!
```

## 4. Paid Alternatives (If you don't want to test 15 apps)
If you are short on time and prefer to just pay for testers:
*   **Fiverr:** Search "20 android testers". Usually costs $10 - $20. They will handle everything and guarantee the 14-day retention.
*   **Dedicated websites:** 20testers.com or testergroup.com provide guaranteed opt-ins.
