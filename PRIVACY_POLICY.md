# Privacy Policy for OutWeather / Microweather

**Last Updated:** March 2026

This privacy policy explains how OutWeather ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

We are committed to ensuring your privacy and protecting the data you share with us.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Location Data
Our core feature is providing hyper-local weather forecasts and activity scores. To do this, the Service requests access to your device's precise (GPS) and approximate location data:
*   **Foreground Location:** Used when the app is open to load the weather for your immediate surroundings.
*   **Background Location (Optional):** If you enable daily background notifications or widget updates, we securely request your location in the background to fetch updated reports without you needing to open the app.

Location data is sent directly to our third-party weather data providers (e.g., Open-Meteo) strictly for the purpose of retrieving meteorological data. **We do not store your location history on our servers or sell your location data to third-party data brokers.**

### 1.2 Usage and Crash Data
To improve the stability of our app, we collect anonymized crash reports and performance data using Sentry. This data includes device type, operating system version, and the stack trace of the crash, but contains no personally identifiable information (PII).

### 1.3 Monetization and Advertising
If you use the free version of our app, we may display advertisements via Google AdMob. 
*   **AdMob** may collect advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID or Apple IDFA) and IP addresses to serve personalized or non-personalized ads. 
*   Depending on your geographic location (such as the GDPR zone in Europe), you will be prompted to consent to personalized advertising upon launching the app.
*   You can read more about how Google uses information from sites or apps that use their services [here](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

### 1.4 Subscriptions
For users who purchase "OutWeather Pro" to unlock premium features, payments are processed securely by RevenueCat and the platform app stores (Google Play Store / Apple App Store). We do not collect, process, or store your credit card information. We retain anonymous, randomly generated user IDs to track subscription entitlements.

## 2. Third-Party Services

We utilize the following third-party services, which have their own privacy policies:
*   **Open-Meteo:** For weather data ([Privacy Policy](https://open-meteo.com/en/features#terms))
*   **Google AdMob:** For advertisement delivery ([Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy))
*   **RevenueCat:** For subscription management ([Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy/))
*   **Sentry:** For crash reporting ([Privacy Policy](https://sentry.io/privacy/))

## 3. Data Retention and Security

We strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your personal information. Any user preferences (such as saved spots or custom card orders) are stored locally on your device using encrypted, secure storage mechanisms and are never transmitted to our servers. 

If you uninstall the app, all locally saved preferences and locations are immediately deleted from your device.

## 4. Children’s Privacy

The Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can take necessary actions.

## 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, please contact us via the support mechanisms provided within the Google Play Store or App Store listing.
